PUMP – Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users of the PUMP mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to iamsaiteja2411@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete Account"
  3. Send the request from the email address associated with your PUMP account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Body metrics and fitness profile data
  • Workout history and logs
  • Profile images
  • AI coaching interaction data

Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

Certain technical logs may be retained for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes. These are automatically deleted after the retention period expires.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receiving the request.
