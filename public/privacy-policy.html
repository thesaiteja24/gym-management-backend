Privacy Policy for PUMP

Last Updated: 13 February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PUMP ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is collected, used, and shared. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application PUMP (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account or update your profile:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, date of birth, gender.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number.
  • Profile Media: Profile picture (uploaded from your device).
  • Authentication Data: Google ID (if logging in via Google).

2.2. Health and Fitness Data

To provide our core fitness tracking and coaching features, we collect:

  • Body Metrics: Height, weight.
  • Fitness Profile: Fitness goals (e.g., gain muscle, lose weight), fitness level (beginner, intermediate, advanced), injuries, and available equipment.
  • Workout Data: Workout logs, exercises performed, sets, reps, weight used, RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion), and workout duration.

2.3. Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with the App:

  • Device Information: IP address, device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Logs: App launch times, features used, and interaction patterns.

2.4. Audio Data

Voice Interactions: When you use the AI Coach feature, we collect and process your voice recordings to transcribe your queries and provide coaching responses.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, track your workouts, and visualize your progress.
  • AI Coaching: Process your voice and text inputs via OpenAI to act as your virtual fitness coach.
  • Personalization: Tailor workout recommendations based on your fitness profile and goals.
  • Communication: Send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Improvement: Analyze usage trends (via Vexo Analytics) to improve App performance and user experience.

4. Permissions We Request

The App requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Camera & Photo Library: To allow you to upload or change your profile picture.
  • Microphone: To enable voice interaction with the AI Coach.

Note: We do not track your precise geolocation.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following third-party service providers who assist in our operations:

Service Provider Purpose Data Shared
Google (Firebase/Auth) Authentication Email, Google ID, Profile generic info
OpenAI AI Coaching Features Voice transcripts, fitness queries, workout context
AWS (Amazon Web Services) File Storage Profile pictures
Vexo Analytics App Analytics Anonymized usage data, device info
Twilio SMS Verification (if applicable) Phone number

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and health data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

Voice Data: Voice recordings are processed for immediate transcription and interaction.
